about me

I am currently finishing up my senior year at the University of Michigan's School of Information. I have greatly enjoyed my schoolwork and am well prepared to start my career in User Experience (UX) Design. As of right now, I am in the process of searching for a job and am excited for my professional career to start.

While in school, I also worked part time as a Web Content Intern for Michigan Engineering's Communications and Marketing Department. In this role, I got lots of experience designing web pages, conducting user research, conducting quality analyses, and more.

Outside of work and school, I love to be outside. I am an avid runner and love adventures of all kind. Additionally, I love to read, write, listen to podcasts, draw, and play video games. I am looking forward to having more time to do all of these things once I graduate!
